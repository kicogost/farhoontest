Execution Timeline

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 0 — Tonight / Tomorrow AM
Set up tracking sheet. Make hire/keep decision on DM closer. Brief VA on new SLAs. Align on DM script (keep or tweak current optimised version).
Day 1–2 — Ramp Up
Go live with new process. Francisco monitors DM closer in real-time to ensure speed-to-lead and volume targets are hit. Nightly report to Farhoon with lead count, convos started, and closes.
Day 3–4 — Optimise
Review first data. Identify drop-off points (e.g., lead replies but doesn't convert — script issue? Objection handling?). A/B test CTA or lead magnet if volume is low.
Day 5–6 — Push Volume
Double down on what's working. Re-engage cold leads from previous weeks who were never contacted. Increase follow-up cadence on warm leads.
Day 7 — Review & Decide
Full debrief with Farhoon. Present dashboard: leads, conversations, closes, revenue, speed-to-lead. Decision: scale, iterate, or revert.
